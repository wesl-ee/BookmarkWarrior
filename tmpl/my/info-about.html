About Us

Bookmark Warrior is a "read-it-later" service which allows you to save bookmarks and interesting articles. Think of us like a bookshelf for things you find on the Internet.

You might find Bookmark Warrior useful for:

  • Articles you start reading at work and want to continue at home
  • Gathering resources and academic articles for a thesis / report
  • Sites you find on your smartphone and want to continue browsing later
  • Online documentation which is relevent to your current projects

If you don't already have an account you can create an account in less than a minute.

History

The first version of Bookmark Warrior was written around January/February 2020; it aimed to upend social bookmarking sites like Reddit, Pocket and Pinboard. We are entirely user-supported with zero recurring costs; we'll never run ads or ask for recurring membership fees.

Bookmark Warrior is written, developed, maintained and operated by me, Wesley Coakley; I wrote this software in my senior year of Electrical / Computer engineering at N.C. State; I'd love to hear from you if love this software (or even if you hate it!); you can e-mail me at w@wesleycoakley.com, much love!
